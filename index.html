Our Ongoing Projects

Project Seva

This project provides food and clothing to underprivileged people so that they don't go hungry or suffer without basic needs.

Project Bachpanshala

Aims to give quality education and learning opportunities to children who don't have access to good schooling.

Project Jeev

Focuses on animal welfare, helping stray animals with food, protection, and care.

Project Udaan

Supports women empowerment through skills training, confidence building and helping them become financially independent.

Project Prakriti

Works on environmental conservation, including tree plantation and sustainability awareness programs.
